Yield Max Ferti Optima is an intelligent web-based system designed to help farmers make informed decisions about fertilizer usage. It utilizes machine learning algorithms and agricultural data analysis to recommend the most suitable fertilizers based on soil type, nutrient levels, crop type, and local climate conditions. The goal is to improve crop yield while ensuring environmental sustainability.
